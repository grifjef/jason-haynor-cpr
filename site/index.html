FAQ

Common questions

How much does a class cost?

Standard classes are $60 per person. Group and workplace pricing is quoted to fit your team — just ask when you reach out.

How long does it take?

Most classes run about 1.5–2 hours, with plenty of hands-on practice. You'll be certified the same day.

What does the class cover?

Adult, child & infant CPR, AED (defibrillator) use, and choking relief — the core skills for real emergencies.

Do I get a certification card?

Yes — you leave certified. The exact certification details are confirmed when you book so you get exactly what you or your workplace needs.

Do you teach groups or come on-site?

Absolutely. We train dental offices, gyms, schools, restaurants, daycares and workplaces — hosting your group or coming to you across Clearwater and Pinellas County.

Who teaches the classes?

Jason Haynor, a Clearwater firefighter and top-rated CPR instructor, along with his team of instructors.

Where are you located?

We're based in Clearwater, FL 33764 and serve all of Pinellas County. Your class location is confirmed when you book — join us or have us come to your site.

How do I sign up?

Easiest is to call or text (727) 421-9099. You can also send a request below or message us on Facebook.
