FAQ

Good to know.

How much does a class cost?

Standard classes are $60 per person. Group and workplace pricing is quoted to fit your team.

How long does it take?

About 1.5–2 hours, with plenty of hands-on practice. You're certified the same day.

What does it cover?

Adult, child & infant CPR, AED (defibrillator) use, and choking relief.

Do I get a certification card?

Yes — you leave certified. Exact certification details are confirmed when you book so you get what you or your workplace needs.

Do you teach groups or come on-site?

Absolutely — dental offices, gyms, schools, restaurants, daycares and workplaces, across Clearwater and Pinellas County.

Who teaches the classes?

Jason Haynor, a Clearwater firefighter and top-rated CPR instructor, along with his team.

Where are you located?

Based in Clearwater, FL 33764, serving all of Pinellas County. Your class location is confirmed when you book.
